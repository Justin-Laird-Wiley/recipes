Ingredients:
	- 3 medium beets, peeled and shredded
	- 3 carrots, peeled and shredded
	- 3 medium baking potatoes, peeled and cubed
	- 1 tbsp. vegetable oil
	- 1 medium onion, chopped
	- 1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
	- 3/4 cup water
	- 1/2 medium head cabbage, cored and shredded
	- 1 (8 oz.) can diced tomatoes, drained
	- 3 cloves garlic, minced
	- salt and pepper to taste
	- 1/2 cup sour cream
	- 1 tbsp. parsley, chopped

Directions:
	1.  Bring 2 quarts of water to a boil.  Add beets, carrots, potatoes, and cook
	until tender -- about 15 minutes.  Add cabbage and diced tomatoes.

	2.  Heat the oil in a skillet.  Add onion and cook until tender.  Stir in tomato
	paste and water until well blended.  Transfer to pot.  Add garlic, cover pot, and
	turn off heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.

	3.  Garnish with sour cream and parsley.
